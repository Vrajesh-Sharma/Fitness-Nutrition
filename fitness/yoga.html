Shavasana (Corpse Pose)

Shavasana, also known as Corpse Pose, is one of the most simple yet deeply effective poses for relaxation. It involves lying down on your back, completely still, while focusing on your breath. This asana helps to calm the mind and reduce stress after an active yoga session.

Advantages:

  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves concentration
  • Promotes deep relaxation and better sleep
  • Helps lower blood pressure

Recommended Duration: 5-10 minutes

Balasana (Child's Pose)

Balasana is a resting pose that helps to stretch the back and relax the entire body. It's great for calming the mind and relieving fatigue.

Advantages:

  • Releases tension in the back and shoulders
  • Calms the mind
  • Stretches hips, thighs, and ankles
  • Relieves fatigue and stress

Recommended Duration: 2-5 minutes

Viparita Karani (Legs-Up-The-Wall Pose)

This pose allows the legs to rest against a wall while lying on the back. It helps improve circulation and relieves tired legs and feet.

Advantages:

  • Improves blood circulation
  • Relieves tired legs and feet
  • Calms the mind
  • Reduces lower back tension

Recommended Duration: 5-15 minutes

Supta Baddha Konasana (Reclining Bound Angle Pose)

This asana opens the hips, stretches the groin, and promotes a deep state of relaxation by encouraging deep breathing.

Advantages:

  • Opens the hips
  • Promotes relaxation
  • Stimulates abdominal organs
  • Helps in stress relief

Recommended Duration: 5-10 minutes

Uttanasana (Standing Forward Bend)

This pose involves bending forward and reaching for the ground, which helps stretch the spine and hamstrings while promoting relaxation.

Advantages:

  • Stretches the hamstrings
  • Improves flexibility
  • Relieves stress and tension
  • Calms the mind

Recommended Duration: 1-3 minutes

Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose)

Setu Bandhasana involves lifting the hips towards the ceiling while lying on your back, promoting flexibility and calming the mind.

Advantages:

  • Stretches the chest, neck, and spine
  • Strengthens back muscles
  • Calms the brain
  • Reduces anxiety, fatigue, and insomnia

Recommended Duration: 3-5 minutes
